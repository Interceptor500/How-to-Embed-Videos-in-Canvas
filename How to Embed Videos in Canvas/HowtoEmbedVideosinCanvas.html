What will make embedding files easy on speech day:

  • Bring a laptop with a webcam, this is by far going to involve the least amount of steps to actually embed your video into the canvas discussion. It will also be much easier to set up the camera.
  • The canvas mobile app doesn’t allow you to embed files. By using a laptop with a webcam, the file is already on the laptop, and can be embedded using the canvas webpage.
  • If for whatever reason you do not have a laptop, there are some alternatives listed in this guide.
  • You should try each method in the order of the chapters.

(1)Laptops with Webcams

  • (1.1)Kaltura

Step 1: Go to the canvas discussion where you want to embed the video

Step 2: Click on “Embed Kaltura media”

        Step 3: Select “Add New” Then select “Media Upload”

        

Step 4: Add your video, then select “Save and Embed”

If you are having trouble finding your video file, you should make sure you are looking in folders labeled as “Videos” or “Camera”

  • (1.2)Alternative to Kaltura

        If Kaltura did not work, you can try to use the other embed tool built into canvas.

        Step 1: Go to the canvas discussion where you want to embed the video.

        Step 2: Click on “Upload/Record Media”

        Step 3: Click to browse your device. Find your video and select it to upload it.

        Step 4: Click submit to embed the video.

(2)Mobile Devices

If you only have a mobile device, methods (1.1) and (1.2) can work with some extra steps. You will need to access canvas from your mobile device’s browser, and view the desktop site. Here is a video of how to do this on Android with Chrome. It may be slightly different on other OS’ but should be quite similar.

(3)YouTube Methods

If you have a YouTube account, you can upload your video to YouTube and embed the video in canvas.

First you must upload your video to YouTube, (3.4) has steps for mobile, and (3.5) has steps for a desktop/laptop.

  • (3.3)Finding Your Unlisted YouTube Video on Desktop/Laptop

Go to YouTube, in the upper right click on your profile picture and select “View your channel.” From here, click on “Manage videos” This allows you to access your unlisted video.

  • (3.4)Uploading to YouTube on Mobile:

Step 1: Open the Youtube app.

Step 2: Click the “+” at the very bottom.

Step 3: At the very bottom scroll to “Video”

Step 4: Select your video and click “Next”

Step 5: Under “Visibility” it will say either Public, Unlisted, or Private. Make sure you select “Unlisted” or “Public”

Step 6: Create a title, then click “Next”

Step 7: YouTube will ask if this video is made for kids, this is if you would like your video   to be visible on the YouTube kids website/app. It is fine to select either choice here.

Step 8: Select upload video.

  • (3.5) Uploading to YouTube on Desktop/Laptop

Step 1: In the upper right, select “Create” Then select “Upload Video”

Step 2: Click “Select Files”

Step 3: Find your video and select it.

Step 4: Choose a title and refer to step 7 of “uploading to youtube on mobile”

Step 5: Click “Next” until you reach the “Visibility” section. Make sure to select “Unlisted” or “Public”

Step 6: Click “Save”
